# Problem Statement 1: Design a Digital Solution for Senior Citizens

Develop a web or mobile application that addresses a specific need or challenge faced by senior citizens. This could relate to health, social connection, daily tasks, safety, or general well-being. You are required to actually talk to users to identify genuine pain points and propose features that directly respond to those needs. Your app should demonstrate empathy for the user group and take into account usability, accessibility, and simplicity.
